## 2. What LineLure Is
LineLure is an arcade puzzle game for mobile devices.  
The app lets users play quick stages, store local settings and scores, and display ads.

Purchases, billing, and refunds are handled exclusively by Apple App Store / Google Play under their own terms.

## 6. What Data We Process
### 6.1 Data handled by the app itself (on your device)
- Best score and local progress.
- Preferences (for example, theme and haptic settings).
- Local ad-removal purchase state cache.

This data is stored locally using on-device storage (`SharedPreferences`).

### 6.2 Data handled by integrated third parties
When using AdMob and app stores, third parties may process technical data, such as:
- Device or advertising identifiers.
- Approximate IP address and network/device information.
- Diagnostics and ad performance information.
- Purchase/restore events processed by Apple/Google.

LineLure does not require account creation and does not request personal identity fields to play.
