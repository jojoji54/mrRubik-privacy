**Last updated: February 23, 2026**

## 2. What StorePulse Is
StorePulse is an analytics and reporting app for App Store Connect metrics.
It helps users connect their App Store Connect account data, review KPI trends, export reports, and manage encrypted backups.

## 3. Platforms and Scope
StorePulse may be available on iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and Android.
This policy applies to all supported builds unless a platform-specific notice states otherwise.

## 4. Age and Acceptable Use
StorePulse is intended for a general audience.
If you are under legal age, use the app under parent or guardian supervision.
Users agree to use the app lawfully and not to tamper with, decompile, reverse engineer, or misuse it.

## 5. In-App Purchases
StorePulse may offer premium features through in-app purchases.
At the time of this policy update, the implemented product is:
- `storepulse_premium_lifetime` (one-time, non-consumable unlock)

Purchases, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play under their own terms.
StorePulse does not directly process card data.

## 6. Data We Process
### 6.1 Data you provide
- App Store Connect credentials entered by you, such as:
  - Issuer ID
  - Key ID
  - Private Key (.p8 content)
  - Vendor Number

### 6.2 Data generated and stored on device
- App settings and preferences (for example theme, security options, filters, and dashboard preferences)
- Cached metric snapshots and technical logs (if enabled)
- Premium unlock local state
- Encrypted backup payloads created by the user

### 6.3 Data handled by platform services
When purchases or restore are used, Apple or Google may process:
- Transaction and purchase metadata
- Store account identifiers required by the platform

When App Store Connect sync is used, requests are sent to Apple endpoints required to retrieve your metrics.

StorePulse does not require user account registration in our own backend.

## 7. Purpose of Processing
We process data to:
- Connect to App Store Connect and retrieve your selected metrics
- Render dashboards, analytics, and reports
- Export CSV, JSON, and PDF files
- Enable encrypted backup export/import workflows
- Apply security features (biometric lock, idle lock, privacy screen)
- Provide premium purchase and restore flows
- Diagnose app issues through optional local technical logs

## 8. Legal Basis
